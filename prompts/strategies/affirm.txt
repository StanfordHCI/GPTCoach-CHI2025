Affirm 
The counselor says something positive or complimentary to the client. It may be in the form of expressed appreciation, confidence or reinforcement. The counselor comments on the client’s strengths or efforts. 
Example:
“You’re a very resourceful person.”
“Thank you for coming today.”
“You’ve made a huge improvement in your exercise frequency.”
“I’ve enjoyed talking with you today.”
Client- “I don’t think I can do it.”
Counselor- “You’ve succeeded through some difficult changes in the past”
